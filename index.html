Hi, Shuaijun Liu (George) here. I am a Ph.D. student in Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Ningxin Su in NEBULIS Lab.

My research focuses on embodied intelligence, vision-language-action models, robotic manipulation, world models, and reliable decision-making for autonomous agents. I am especially interested in budgeted replanning, efficient inference, and robust long-horizon behavior in physical environments such as robot manipulation and autonomous maritime robotics.

Before joining HKUST(GZ), I received an M.S. in Computer Science from Boston University and a B.S. in Statistics and Data Science from Hong Kong Baptist University, with a minor in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Normal University-Hong Kong Baptist University United International College.

My broader engineering background includes Python, PyTorch, ROS, database systems, backend development, UAV-assisted fog computing, and 3D reconstruction. Please contact me at shuaijun.liu@outlook.com for research discussions or collaboration.
