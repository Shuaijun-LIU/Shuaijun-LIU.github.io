RESEARCH
======
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

__Research Interests:__
  * Embodied intelligence and reliable autonomous agents.
  * Vision-language-action models for robotic manipulation.
  * Budgeted replanning, world models, and efficient inference for long-horizon embodied tasks.

<div style="display: flex; justify-content: space-between;">
    <div style="text-align: left;"><strong>When Replanning Becomes the Bottleneck: Budgeted Replanning for Embodied Agents</strong></div>
    <div style="text-align: right;">11/2025 - 04/2026</div>
</div>
* __Key Words:__ Embodied AI, Replanning, Efficient Inference, Token Budgeting, Latency SLOs
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
  <li><strong>Overview:</strong> This work studies the replanning overhead of long-horizon embodied agents and proposes BRACE, a budgeted replanning framework that adaptively decides whether to replan, chooses a replanning mode, and allocates token and latency budgets. It also introduces E-RECAP, a context compression module that preserves task-critical information while reducing inference latency and token consumption.</li>
</ul>
* __Publication:__ __S. Liu__, F. You, X. Chen, and N. Su\*, "When Replanning Becomes the Bottleneck: Budgeted Replanning for Embodied Agents," _ICML 2026_, accepted.

    <div style="text-align: right;">11/2025 - 04/2026</div>
</div>
* __Key Words:__ VLA Models, Robot Manipulation, Semantic Robustness, Action Consistency
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
  <li><strong>Overview:</strong> This research studies action reliability under appearance shifts and task-semantic changes. The goal is to keep actions stable when task semantics remain unchanged, while producing correct action separation when goals, constraints, or execution order change.</li>
</ul>

    <div style="text-align: left;"><strong>Data-grounded simulation and benchmarking for marine robotics</strong></div>
    <div style="text-align: right;">11/2025 - 04/2026</div>
</div>
* __Key Words:__ Marine Robotics, Simulation, Benchmarking, Autonomous Agents
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
  <li><strong>Overview:</strong> This project builds a data-driven simulation and evaluation suite for marine robots by integrating bathymetry, currents, tides, and pollution-field information.
